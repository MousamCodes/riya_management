What is GST Registration?

GST, or Goods and Services Tax, is a comprehensive tax levied on the supply of goods and services. GST registration is mandatory for businesses with an annual turnover above a certain threshold or for those engaged in interstate sales. Obtaining a GSTIN (GST Identification Number) is the first step towards tax compliance, enabling your business to collect GST on sales, claim input tax credit, and file tax returns. Our service ensures this process is smooth and hassle-free, saving you valuable time and effort.

Required Documents for GST Registration

  • PAN Card of the applicant
  • Aadhaar Card of the applicant
  • Business Proof (e.g., Company Registration Certificate)
  • Proof of Business Address (e.g., electricity bill, rent agreement)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Passport-sized photographs
