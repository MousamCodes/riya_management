Professional Birth Certificate Application & Correction Services

Your birth certificate is a crucial document. We offer expert guidance to simplify the application, correction, or new registration process, so you can focus on what matters most.

Why is a Birth Certificate so Important?

A birth certificate is not just a document; it's the first official proof of your existence. It serves as a foundational identity document required for a wide range of legal and personal purposes, including school admission, passport application, visa processing, Aadhaar card enrollment, and voter ID registration. Ensuring its accuracy is vital, as any errors can cause significant issues in the future.

Our Birth Certificate Services

  • New Birth Certificate Application: We assist with the complete process of applying for a new birth certificate.
  • Birth Certificate Correction: If there are errors in your existing certificate, we provide expert support to get them corrected quickly.
  • Delayed Registration: For births that were not registered within the official timeframe, we guide you through the delayed registration process.
  • Non-Availability of Birth Certificate (NABC): We help you obtain a NABC, an essential document for those who cannot find their original birth record.

Required Documents for Birth Certificate

  • Proof of parent's identity (e.g., Aadhaar card, PAN card)
  • Marriage Certificate of parents (if applicable)
  • Hospital Discharge Summary
  • Application form (we will provide and assist in filling it out)
  • Affidavit (for delayed registrations)
