Fast and Reliable PAN Card Application & Correction Services

A Permanent Account Number (PAN) is essential for all financial transactions in India. We simplify the entire process, from new applications to corrections, ensuring a smooth experience.

What is a PAN Card?

A **PAN card** is a unique, ten-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department of India. It is a mandatory document for filing income tax returns and is widely used as a valid proof of identity. The PAN card links all of a person's financial transactions to their unique number, preventing tax evasion and ensuring transparency in the financial system. Whether you need to open a bank account, invest, or file taxes, a valid PAN card is a must-have.

Our Comprehensive PAN Card Services

  • New PAN Card Application: We assist with the complete process of applying for a new PAN card for individuals, companies, and trusts.
  • PAN Card Correction: If there are errors in your existing card (e.g., name, address, date of birth), we provide expert support to get them corrected quickly.
  • Duplicate PAN Card: We guide you through the process of applying for a duplicate PAN card if yours is lost or damaged.
  • Aadhaar-PAN Linking: We ensure your Aadhaar card is correctly linked with your PAN to comply with government regulations.

Required Documents for PAN Card Application

  • Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport)
  • Proof of address (e.g., electricity bill, rent agreement)
  • Proof of date of birth (e.g., birth certificate, matriculation certificate)
  • Passport-sized photographs
